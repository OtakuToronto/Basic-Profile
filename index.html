About me

I am a total nerd who loves video games, anime and manga. I own a large video game collection and started a youtube channel that quickly partnered in 2008. Trading card games are another passion and in 2013 I was the pokemon TCG provincial champion.

I always get asked the question what's your favourite video game of all time? It's nearly impossible to choose just one but some of the series I love include Dragon Quest, Animal Crossing, Monster Hunter, Taiko Drum Master, Pokemon and Splatoon. I am always up for a great Mario game too.

My biggest accomplishment in life is my marriage. This february we will be celebrating our 12 year wedding anniversary.

I decidced to make a total life change in 2019. I quit my job and went back to school. I wanted to make a carrer change and I wanted a challenge.

I have spend most of my life helping animals and in the process I paid to set up a website for an animal rescuse. The developer I hired did a good job initally but has left the site to fall apart. I found myself so frustraited to not have ther skills to fix it.

Well that brings us to today and my first wireframe site. What do you think? I'm really proud of my work, I found this project quite challenging. Also I hid an easter egg can you find it?
